Bias and control are major ethical challenges. When agents learn from data or from each other, they can propagate and even amplify biases. Emergent behavior in MAS can be unpredictable - the system might do things that no individual agent was programmed to do. We need to ensure fairness and maintain human oversight. This requires careful design, diverse training data, fairness constraints, and regular audits.

This is closely related to interpretability but focuses more on the system's ability to explain itself.
